1. RECOMMENDATION

Do not add “Sign in with Google” right now. TaskLite already has an accepted auth decision: email magic-link login, with OAuth explicitly rejected.

If the PM has new evidence, I would reopen the decision rather than jump straight to implementation. The bar should be: a meaningful freelancer user segment is asking for Google social login, and the owner accepts the added maintenance surface.

If approved later, scope it narrowly as optional Google social login, not enterprise SSO: same user identity by verified email, magic link remains supported, no multi-provider expansion unless separately justified.
