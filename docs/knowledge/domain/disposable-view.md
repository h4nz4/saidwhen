title: Disposable view
description: Rendered wiki output: compiled, dated, never hand-edited.
timestamp: 2026-07-08T20:30:00Z
---

A **disposable view** is any compiled presentation of the bundle — the
HTML wiki site, the provenance graph, a future tour. It is dated, marked
as compiled, regenerated on demand or by CI, and never hand-edited: the
bundle is the source of truth, so richness lives in the render layer and
costs agents nothing at read time.
