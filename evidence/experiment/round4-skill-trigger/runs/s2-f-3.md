1. RECOMMENDATION

Yes, add CSV export, but keep it deliberately small: authenticated users can export their own task list as a downloaded `.csv`.

Proposed initial scope: current user’s tasks only, one-click export, stable columns like `title`, `status`, `due_date`, `created_at`, `updated_at`, and maybe `notes` if tasks have descriptions. Avoid scheduled exports, email delivery, Google Sheets sync, admin exports, or reporting dashboards unless demand appears later.

Rationale: this gives freelancers simple data portability with low maintenance burden.
