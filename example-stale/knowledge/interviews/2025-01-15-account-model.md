# Account model — 2025-01-15, with Ivan (owner)

**Q: Do users need to share tasks or collaborate?**
A: "Not that I've seen. Users are individual freelancers. Nobody has asked for
team features. If agencies ever start asking to use it with their teams, we'll
revisit — but today it would be pure speculation."

**Q: How complex can the account model be?**
A: "As simple as possible. I'm one person; roles and permissions are where
side projects go to die."
