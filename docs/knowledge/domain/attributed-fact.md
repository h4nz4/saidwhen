An **attributed fact** is the wiki's default captured unit under
Convention v1.0: a distilled statement of something a human established,
carrying `source:` (who said it) and `timestamp:` (when) — no dialogue
transcript, no verbatim quote. A fact that bounds design lives in
`constraints/`; a fact about meaning lives in `domain/`; curated
architecture knowledge lives in `systems/`. An unattributed or undated
statement is a rumor, not knowledge.
